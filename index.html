Measuring a design system

Why? What? How?

Why to measure?

  • Design system needs to prove its business value to the upper levels. If the value is measurable, it can result into better management of resources.
  • Design system team needs confirmation if they are doing the right thing.
  • Design system team needs to correct its plans according to instant measurements and dynamic picture.

Valuable for different levels

  • (B) Measuring business value What is important for the business levels to know
  • (P) Measuring system-project interactions How good the design system is for its users
  • (I) Internal measuring Workload, internal costs, etc

Costs and savings

  • Component needs to be supported
  • We support only one component

Costs formula, per component

How much a component costs

$ = production time
    + (support costs * component lifetime)
    + (integration time * amount of projects)
